FACEMELTER


Due to an incident at his old workplace involving the consumption of a special meatball, he became the world's smartest man. Even though his actions may seem insane, remember that, at the end of the day, he is smarter than everyone else.

SERUTUS


Fitting the description of a pirate, he uses his gauntlet to travel through time and universes, aiding help in fighting against his mysterious enemy.

ZUZIA


A cat furry trying to live through all the hate. She may not be the wisest, but she definitely has a good heart. She tries to be very human, she even learned their language!

LUDWIG


An ex-criminal who was turned into a monster as a punishment. He worked with Facemelter at the same place before. A noble scientist.

JELENA


A cute, furry reindeer with an attachment disorder that craves cuddles and love. Facemelter's ex-girlfriend.

GREG


The incarnation of technology. Spawned in to help humans, gets invited by Serutus for assistance.

EVADER


The mysterious (and rich) archenemy, needing the toughest team to even try harming him.
