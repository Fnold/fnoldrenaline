120 MINUTES, NO TAKESIES-BACKSIES

AT LEAST 1 PAGE PER DAY


Each day during development, at least one full page had to be completed, adding another layer of challenge to the project.

ONLY KNOWN CHARACTERS


No new characters were used in the comic ― only familiar, already known faces here!
